Snooker

NEIL ROBERTSON

Top Snooker Pro Neil Robertson is the first Australian to ever win a ranking tournament. He is a formal World Champion winning the World Championship title in 2010. His game just gets better and better. His long-potting, safety play and break building have come a long way in recent years. He is one of the strongest young players in the game today. On 1 April 2010 Robertson made the first official maximum break of his career in his second round match in the 2010 China Open against Peter Ebdon,proving that he’s right up there amongst the best in the world, and now a member of the ‘147 Club’. Some have noted that his speed of play has slowed since 2010
